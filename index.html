User Guide

Everything you need to know to get the most out of mAgicmiRror

System Requirements

  • Android 7.0 (API level 24) or higher
  • ARCore supported device
  • Minimum 3GB RAM
  • 500MB available storage
  • Rear camera with autofocus

Setup Instructions

  1. Download and install the mAgicmiRror APK
  2. Grant camera and storage permissions when prompted
  3. Download the companion digital textbook
  4. Ensure good lighting conditions for optimal scanning
  5. Allow the app to download AR models on first launch

Best Practices

  • Hold your device steady when scanning images
  • Ensure the target image is well-lit and clearly visible
  • Keep the textbook flat on a stable surface
  • Move slowly when exploring 3D models
  • Use headphones for the best audio experience

Troubleshooting

  • App won't scan: Check camera permissions and lighting
  • 3D model not appearing: Ensure ARCore is installed and updated
  • Slow performance: Close other apps and restart mAgicmiRror
  • Audio issues: Check device volume and audio permissions
